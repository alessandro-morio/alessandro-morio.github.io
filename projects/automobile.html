Automobile sector - PowerKPI Service and Service/Event Monitoring

My customer Urban Science provides an excellent automotive market analysis software to automobile manufacturers. Urban Science is on the market for over 30 years and works with all noteworthy car manufacturers.
I worked on the new so called PowerKPI service which is a highly configurable dataset/KPI processing service.
Additonally I extended the existing SystemAdmin service which is responsible for triggering and monitoring events of various services.

Technical Implementation and Personal Contribution

The services I worked on are written in modern C# 5/6 ASP.Net Core with an Angular frontend managed in a NX monorepo. There are various micro-services with different domain responsibilities involved. The clean architecture makes working with these services a great pleasure.
